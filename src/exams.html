The Certification Programs by Microsoft

Along with its top-tier advancements, the company also runs a high-quality certification program. This essentially helps individuals turn into qualified administrators and skilled technicians. It successfully helps to grow to support its system and various application products.

The many certifications within the certification program of Microsoft comprise of the following:

  • Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (MCSA)
  • Microsoft Solutions Developer (MCSD)
  • Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA)
  • Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert (MCSE)
  • Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS)

MCSA is a program for certification which is essential for individuals seeking for entry-level positions in any IT sector. It is referred to as a prerequisite for the upper levels of certifications offered by Microsoft. The MCSA comprises of many specialty areas, which involves MCSA cloud track, along with MCSA mobility, and even MCSA data certifications.

MCSE is a program for individuals who have passed the certification exam based on the Microsoft Windows NT operating system, Microsoft BackOffice server products, related desktop systems, and networking. MCSE for cloud platform and infrastructure requires individuals to qualify MCSA. Following which, students have to pass one of the 15 exams to pass the certification.
